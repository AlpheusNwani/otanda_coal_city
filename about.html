About OTANDA COAL CITY LIMITED

Building trust through land, construction, and property solutions

OTANDA COAL CITY LIMITED is a reputable multi-service property and urban planning firm operating across Nigeria. We specialize in real estate sales and marketing, physical planning, building approvals and planning schemes, town and urban planning services, and layout design development.

Driven by professionalism, integrity, and excellence, we provide reliable and affordable solutions tailored to individuals, investors, government agencies, and corporate clients seeking sustainable development and long-term value.

Mission

To provide reliable and affordable property and construction solutions that meet the needs of clients across Nigeria.

Vision

To become a leading real estate and construction brand in Africa known for excellence and trust.
